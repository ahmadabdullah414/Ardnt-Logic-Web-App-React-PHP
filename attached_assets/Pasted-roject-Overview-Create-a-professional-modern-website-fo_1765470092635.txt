roject Overview:
Create a professional, modern website for ArdntLogic, a digital agency offering $299/month Local Business Packages. The website will be used to attract local businesses in various niches (construction, property management, restaurants, etc.). The agency’s goal is to help these businesses grow online through services like website design, SEO, lead generation, review management, and more.

Accent Color: Neon (Teal/Orange) – used for buttons, CTAs, hover effects to stand out

Key Features of the Website:

SEO-Friendly Structure

Clear Call-to-Actions (CTA) throughout the site
